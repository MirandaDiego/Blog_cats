Could cats be astronauts?

Cats are fascinating animals and surrounded by mysteries. Independent in temperament and mysterious, these felines are known for their agility, elegance and a lots of sometimes unpredictable. However, recently, a theory has gained space on the internet and among Conspiracy theorists: Are cats astronauts?
The theory suggests that cats are not terrestrial animals, but extraterrestrial beings sent to carry out a specific mission on our planet. There are historical reports that point to O fact that cats were considered sacred beings in different cultures and even associated with divine figures. This could indicate that cats have a much more mystique than previously thought.

Furthermore, there are theorists who believe that felines have telepathic abilities and are capable of communicating with other alien species. This belief is based on reports of people who claim to have seen cats reacting to stimuli that are not perceptible to their beings humans, or even behaving unusually during astronomical events, such as one Northern Lights. These and other signs were interpreted as signs that cats would be interplanetary beings.
